~

Change of a character in a document.

Examples:

5~ will change tHe following 5 five characters from the cursor
Ctrl + V5 WILL change the case of the line
g~iw will change the case of the word under the cursor

Bonus: U will always change case to uppercase while ~ will "toggle"

Bonus2: u will always change case to lowercase

Bonus3: As usual doubling the commmand will apply it to the current line. Like gUU, guu, g~~ etc
