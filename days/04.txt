~

Change case of a character in a document.

Examples:

5~ will change tHe following 5 five characters from the cursor
Ctrl + V5 WILL change the case of the line
g~iw will change the case of the word under the cursor

Bonus: U in visual model will always change case to uppercase while ~ will
"toggle"

Bonus 2: u in visual will always change case to lowercase

Bonus 3: As usual doubling the commmand will apply it to the current line. Like
gUU, guu, g~~ etc
