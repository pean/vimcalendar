f{char}

Go to the next occurrence of {char} in the current line.

Bonus: F will find to the left of the cursor

Bonus 2: t/T will do the same thing but put the cursor in front of the character

Bonus 2: t/T will do the same thing but put the cursor in front of the character

Bonus 3: ; repeats the latest f, t, F, or T.

Bonus 4: , repeats the latest f, t, F or T but in the opposite direction

Bonus 5: cf{char} will delete all characters up until and including {char} and enter insert mode
