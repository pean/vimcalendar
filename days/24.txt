q{0-9a-zA-Z}

This will record everthing you are doing. This can be used to repeat tasks and
and a repetetive task can be created by replaying the recording before ending it.

For example, q1fsra@1 will replace all occurences of s with a on a single line.

q stops the recording.

@{0-9a-z".=*+} will replay your recording.

@@ will repeat the previous replayed recording.

Bonus: @@ [count] will replay [count] number of times.
